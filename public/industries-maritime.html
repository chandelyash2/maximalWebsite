Our preventive measures include screening of vehicles and trucks entering the restricted port and harbor area, screening of pedestrians entering the restricted area, baggage and container screening with an x-ray tool, site access control before the restricted area, closure of entrances outside docking period, and video surveillance with alarm verification and call of forces if needed.
