A Trusted Partner in Retail Security

Our retail security solution goes way beyond conventional alarm panels focusing on sending alarms to our clients. By combining our knowledge with the latest technologies, we give our clients real-time control over their entire business. Our clients need 24/7 surveillance to cover loitering in the parking lot, an increased number of incidents at the stores and unwanted wandering around at night. In addition, special events organized by the clients need extra security services. With an easily deployed video surveillance device all incidents are instantly reported based on intelligent video analytics. The Maximal Operation Center alerts remote guarding officers who analyze the live video and sends out the mobile guarding officers in the local area of the site.
