Proprietary Security Technology

Maximal Security blends human vigilance with intelligent technology. From digital reports and guest screening to team messaging and object recognition, our tools work alongside our professionals, amplifying their insight and ensuring your property and people are protected.

At Maximal, we don’t just provide highly trained security officers — we build technology that enhances performance, streamlines communication, and ensures full transparency for our clients. Our proprietary systems are designed with flexibility in mind and continuously evolve to meet the unique needs of the organizations we serve.

The Maximal Reporting System is a state-of-the-art platform designed to streamline and enhance reporting for physical security operations. It enables the creation of customized report templates, which can be assigned to security officers for daily completion. Reports are updated in real-time and are immediately accessible to both Maximal’s management team and our clients.

Key features include:

  • Accessible via both web and mobile applications.
  • Time-stamped entries ensure timely task completion and officer attentiveness.
  • Supports various input types including images, checkboxes, numerical fields, text, tables, and more.
  • Fully exportable to PDF and Excel formats.
  • Reports can be shared instantly via email, SMS, or other communication platforms.

The Maximal Visitor & Employee Management System offers a comprehensive solution for tracking the flow of personnel through a facility. It enhances security and accountability while streamlining front-desk operations.

Key features include:

  • Captures visitor photos, prints badges, and supports efficient check-in/check-out processes.
  • Alerts client employees upon visitor check-in.
  • Logs, tracks, and assigns mail and deliveries to designated recipients.
  • Instantly notifies recipients upon delivery arrival.
  • Provides clients with real-time visitor logs and historical data.

The Maximal Object Recognition System leverages advanced recognition technology to automate key security functions. It increases operational efficiency, minimizes manual oversight, and proactively prevents unauthorized access.

Example capabilities include:

  • Grants access when an authorized individual’s face is recognized.
  • Automatically opens gates or barriers upon recognizing an approved license plate.
  • Detects unauthorized vehicles or blacklisted individuals and instantly alerts on-site personnel.

Maximal Messenger is a secure, real-time communication platform that connects clients directly with their assigned security team and Maximal management. It ensures fast, reliable coordination and instant responsiveness.

Platform benefits:

  • Enables clients to send live updates, alerts, or instructions to on-site personnel.
  • Allows Maximal management to communicate directly with assigned officers.
  • • Officers acknowledge and confirm receipt of instructions, ensuring clarity and accountability.

The Maximal Guard Management System simplifies scheduling, tracking, and monitoring of security personnel. It provides both Maximal staff and clients with real-time access to schedules via mobile and web platforms.

Core features:

  • Efficiently manages and displays weekly schedules for each site.
  • Utilizes geofencing to verify on-time arrival and ongoing presence throughout shifts.
  • Sends instant alerts for late arrivals, mid-shift departures, or pre-shift absences from the designated site.
  • Promotes transparency, reliability, and continuous protection.

At Maximal, we are committed to evolving our technology to meet the specific needs of each client. We actively welcome feedback and offer tailored solutions to address your organization’s unique security requirements.
