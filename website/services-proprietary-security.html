Cutting-Edge Security Technology – Tailored to Protect Your People, Property, Assets, and Operations

Maximal Security blends human vigilance with intelligent technology. From digital reports and guest screening to team messaging and object recognition, our tools work alongside our professionals, amplifying their insight and ensuring your property and people are protected.

  • Highly customizable report templates tailored to client needs.
  • Administrators can assign templates to officers for completion.
  • Reports support multiple formats: pictures, checkboxes, numbers, text fields, tables, and more.
  • Each entry is timestamped, ensuring accountability and proof that officers were alert and executing tasks on schedule.
  • Reports are viewable live as they are populated, accessible via web or mobile app.
  • All reports are fully exportable to PDF and Excel, sharable instantly through email, text message, or other communication channels.

  • Tracks both visitors and employees entering/exiting facilities.
  • Captures photos, prints badges, and supports check-in/check-out workflows.
  • Mail handling and deliveries can be logged, tracked, and assigned to recipients.
  • Recipients are instantly notified when their delivery arrives.
  • Enhances lobby/front desk security and improves accountability.
  • Provides clients with real-time logs and visitor history reports.

  • Automates security functions based on recognition triggers.
  • Grants access inside the building when authorized personnel’s face is recognized.
  • Opens property gate or boom barrier when authorized vehicle license plate is recognized.
  • Identifies unauthorized vehicles and blacklisted individuals attempting to enter the property and sends notification to property personnel.
  • Improves efficiency, reduces reliance on manual verification, and prevents unauthorized access.

  • A secure communication platform similar to top industry messaging apps.
  • Each client site has dedicated chat groups for their assigned officers and management.
  • Clients can send real-time requests, updates, or alerts directly to security personnel.
  • Enables instant, secure, and documented communication.

  • Allows management to efficiently schedule guards at client sites.
  • Uses geofencing to confirm arrival on-time and presence throughout the shift.
  • Notifications are triggered if:
    • A guard fails to arrive on time.
    • A guard leaves the site during an active shift.
    • A guard is not within geographic reach prior to shift start.
  • Ensures accountability, reliability, and uninterrupted protection.

We continue to expand our technology offerings and refine existing systems to adapt to client requirements. Our development team welcomes feedback and can create or enhance solutions to address specific security requirements of your organization.
