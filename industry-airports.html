A Trusted Partner in Airport Security

Whether you’re looking to safeguard your airport, your aircrafts, or to control access to airside areas, we will handle your security needs with a customer-centric approach. Our flexible workforce of security guards is expertly trained to secure your airport in a variety of ways.

Our core services include patrolling the primary security line, the airport terminal buildings, the airside ramp areas and other facilities, 24/7, on foot and by mobile patrol; Managing crowds and control traffic; Providing customer service to passengers in and around the airport; Monitoring and control access to restricted areas of the airport; Managing the 24/7 Security Operations Control Center (SOCC) and actively review CCTV footage and ground-based radars; Responding to security breaches, airfield emergencies, and active threats (bomb threats, terrorist attacks, active assailant and active shooter), as well as medical emergencies; Providing ongoing training to security personnel including Control Tactics and De-Escalation Techniques, while ensuring all new recruits are highly trained; Enforcing airside traffic directives and ensure that airport security personnel also staff airport Pass Control Offices.
